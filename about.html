DEAF Ed

It is an expert mobile application that guides the hearing and non-hearing impaired to an effective communication.

  • Encourages interaction and discussion between deaf and able
  • Educate people in using and understanding basics in sign language.
  • Help children who requires extra effort expressing their ideas or emotions
  • To prevent people for causing feelings of loneliness, isolation and frustration particularly among older people with this incapacity
  • To help them participate in the community
